timeline How I Got Here

The journey so far

It's not a straight line, but every detour taught me something.

~2016

Started Carnatic Music & Veena

Began learning Carnatic music and picked up the veena. Had no idea this would end up shaping my entire approach to learning — but 10 years in, I can say it taught me more about discipline than anything else.

2023

Fell into the Android rabbit hole

Realized you could actually build apps and even change how a phone's entire operating system works. That was it — I was hooked. Started learning, broke a few things, and never looked back.

2024

Started building custom ROMs

Went from just flashing other people's ROMs to actually understanding how they're put together. AOSP, device trees, kernel configs — the whole ecosystem. Steep learning curve, but that's the fun part.

2025

Ported HyperOS 2 to the M34

This was the project that changed everything. Getting Xiaomi's HyperOS 2 to boot on a Samsung Galaxy M34 took serious work — but watching that first boot animation come up on screen was genuinely one of the best moments.

2026

11th class — going all in

Balancing school with everything else, but more focused than ever. Learning to code properly, diving deeper into UI/UX, and continuing veena practice. The goal isn't to be the smartest person in the room — it's to be someone who actually knows what they're doing.
