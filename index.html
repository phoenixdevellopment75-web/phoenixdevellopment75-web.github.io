01 — About

A little bit about who I am

My name's Phoenix, and I'm currently in Class 11 — though if you asked me what I spend most of my time doing, school probably wouldn't be the first thing I mention. I've been studying Carnatic music and learning veena for about 10 years now. That journey taught me something no tutorial ever could: real skill takes patience, repetition, and a genuine willingness to sit with something until you actually get it.

On the tech side, I'm deep into Android — building apps, porting custom ROMs, and designing interfaces. I care about how things work under the hood just as much as how they look on screen. I'm not here to fake expertise or take shortcuts. I want to understand things properly, even if it takes longer.

04 — Journey

How I got here

Started Carnatic Music & Veena

Began learning one of the world's most intricate classical music traditions and picked up the veena. Had no idea it would end up shaping my entire approach to learning.

2023

Fell into the Android rabbit hole

Realized you could build apps and even change how a phone's entire OS works. That was it — I was hooked. Started learning, broke a few things, never looked back.

Went from flashing other people's ROMs to building my own. Ported HyperOS 2 to the Samsung M34 — the project that changed everything for me.

Balancing school with everything else. Learning to code properly, diving deeper into UI/UX, continuing veena. The goal: be someone who actually knows what they're doing.
